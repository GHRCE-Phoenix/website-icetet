ICETET- ISA 2019

International Conference on Emerging Trends in Engineering & Technology on Information Security and Analytics.

Nagpur, Maharashtra
June 1-2 2019


call for papers



BORDERLINE


2nd International Conference on Emerging Trends in Engineering & Technology on Information Security and Analytics, The conference will bring together experts from the information security and information Analytics to discuss the timely issue of information security and Analytics. This will provide a forum for sharing insights, experiences and interaction on various aspects of evolving technologies and patterns related to Computer Science. Authors are solicited to contribute to the conference by submitting articles that illustrate research results, projects, surveying works and industrial experiences that describe significant advances in the following areas, but are not limited to that.



Submission Guidelines


Authors are invited to submit papers through easy chair conference management system
(https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=icisa2018)
by 5th March 2019. Submissions must be original and should not have been published previously or be under consideration for publication while being evaluated for this conference. All papers should be formatted as close as possible to the final format (templates are available on this Web site for your convenience).
Template: (Word format)
The author can use following link for formatting the paper: http://helix.dnares.in/authors/ Regular paper of length 5 are accepted for oral presentations. Rs. 800/- or 15 USD (for international authors) will be charged per page if exceeds 5 pages
