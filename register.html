REGISTRATION POLICY FOR ICETET-ISA 2019

EVERY Registration/TICKET INCLUDES
Each ticket includes entry to the Conference Inaugural and Morning breakfast and Lunch for 1-2 June, 2019
Plus regular coffee and snack breaks, so there is plenty of time to interact with renowned dignitaries.
If you have got any questions, just email us and we will be in touch. See you there!
If you are unable to register online please drop a mail to Chair (Registration opens Jan 30, 2019)
